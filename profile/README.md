## About

The **FBOT Robotics Research Group** is part of the Research Group in Automation and Intelligent Robotics (NAUTEC) at the Federal University of Rio Grande (FURG), Brazil. Our research spans autonomous mobile robotics, manipulation, perception, and intelligent systems, with a strong emphasis on real-world deployment validated through international robotics competitions.
